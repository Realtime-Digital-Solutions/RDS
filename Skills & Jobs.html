Skills & Jobs

Team of 4
(In addition to Project Manager)


 DevOps Engineer

A DevOps Engineer is responsible for the development, maintenance and testing of infrastructure so that software can be successfully deployed. They will work closely with the Full Stack Developer to produce the software needed for the product. They require the ability to problem-solve and multi-task.


Full Stack Developer

A Full Stack Developer is integral to any project. They are programmers who are trained in both front and back-end coding. This person will provide the specific technical expertise required to complete this project. They will need to have great communication skills, as they will need to collaborate with each other member of the project.


Quality Assurance Analyst

The Quality Assurance Analyst is responsible for ensuring that the product is ready to be released. They check the software for any errors or bugs. They will create test plans and work in a team environment to effectively resolve any issues that are found. They require problem-solving skills and great attention to detail.


Business Analyst

The Business Analyst is crucial to the success of the product. They will be responsible for assessing the current market to devise a strategy for a product launch. They will draw on their business management skills to liaise with stakeholders and report on projected growth and business impacts. This person will have experience with working as part of a team and showcasing innovative thinking.
