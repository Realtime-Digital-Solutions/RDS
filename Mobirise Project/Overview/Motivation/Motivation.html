Motivation

The motivation for our Medical IoT project, which we have decided to name “PulseBeat”, came from our sheer interest in helping people. "What area needs more assistance than any others when it comes constant improvement?”
The Health Care industry. Technology has always done great wonders for Health and everyone has someone dear to them that they cherish, who can offer knowledge and wisdom to a younger generation. Our project started to gain traction after a brief discussion within the group that this was of a beneficial stature. The Medical field is a never-ending quest for constant improvement and the research that laboratory professionals and all involved personnel provide to conquer old and new diseases and ailments are miracles in their own mind. If we could relieve the Health System, which is currently overpopulated with patients and reaching capacity limits in providing the appropriate care, as well as resources limited, we would be doing our part in securing a future. Major companies are backed up by financial institutions and the fact that we believe in our product as Version 1.0 is a testament to the perseverance of the Team. Current I.T. trends suggest that there are iterations of similar products out in the market. As with many ideas, someone has already tried to solve a problem. This is one of the key fundamental qualities needed to achieve the goal of being successful, be it personally or financially.

In the never-ending strive for perfection within the technological field, we as a team can only contribute to the many ideas out in force today. Australian Health Care is one of, if not the strongest of the nations in world and it is our duty as citizens to ensure the survival and flourishing of Australians and humans as a whole. We live in a world of IoT (Internet of Things) and nearly every device that is released today, has communication and internet capability among a string of devices all connected. Our product is among many that will try to “solve a problem.”

In accordance with future Employer needs, our team shows great initiative for brainstorming ideas, seeking out the sources needed to sustain the idea, troubleshooting, belief in future proofing versions and working as a team to conquer any discrepancies that we encounter. These are all qualities that employers are searching for when building a team. Programming skills will come in time, with an applied mindset. Our alternate goal as students, is to constantly learn from our mistakes and apply the new education we receive as we strive to become experienced professionals and better people.
